Chewy Sugar Cookies

Description

Sugar cookies that are both crisp on the outside and chewy on the inside.
These cookies can also yield snickerdoodle cookies by rolling the dough in
cinnamon-sugar prior to baking.

Ingredients

  • 2 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 and 1/4 cups margarine
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup white sugar for decoration
